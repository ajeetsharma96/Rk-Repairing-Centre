About Us

RK Repairing Centre is a business founded by two brothers, Rajendra and Subhash, with a passion for repairing and maintaining machines. With years of experience in the industry, the brothers have built a reputation for offering high-quality repair services for various types of machines. We Sell blades and machines and Repair all types of industrial machines. Our team of skilled technicians is dedicated to providing prompt and efficient service to our customers. We are committed to delivering exceptional results and building long-term relationships with our clients . Our goal is to become the go-to repair service provider for industrial machines in the region.

The first branch of RK Repairing Centre was established on Labour Adda Road, near Baba Flour Mill,Krishna Nagar, Yusufguda, Hyderabad, and has been serving the community for many years. Due to growing demand and our commitment to providing excellent service, we opened a second branch near Iyengar's Bakery,Green Bawarchi in Krishna Nagar, Yusufguda, Hyderabad.

Both locations are staffed with experienced professionals who are dedicated to repairing a wide range of machines, including drill machines, polish machines, chipping machines, saw machines, and various cutting machines for tiles, granite, and wood. Our goal is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable services to keep your machines running smoothly.
